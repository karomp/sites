Design Philosophy

My design philosophy in general is to make my works as creative as possible while still maintaining as much accessibility as possible - in practice, it means that while creating and designing, I aim to take into consideration many different disabilities or issues as possible; is the design flow intuitive, is the font readable enough, are the colors and shapes contrasting enough to be clear even with vision issues?

Of course, this doesn't mean that I aim to tone down my own artistic influence on the work; I have no desire to create boring corposlop after all. But I hope some of the work I have showcased here speaks to this philosophy...
- colorful, contrasting, eyecatching art that isn't eyestraining
- a simple, easily navigatable website
- thoughtful, interesting character design
- and of course, varied, unique graphic design
